# Open Trivia

## Tagline
**Challenge your mind with exciting quizzes across categories and climb the global leaderboards.**

## Project Description
Open Trivia is an engaging quiz platform that tests your knowledge across diverse categories. Built with Next.js 15 and Firebase, it offers instant scoring, real-time leaderboards, and personalized user profiles. Challenge yourself with questions from various topics, track your progress, compete with players worldwide, and enjoy a seamless, responsive quiz experience on any device.

## Key Features
- **Diverse Categories**: Explore questions across multiple topics and difficulty levels
- **Real-Time Scoring**: Get instant feedback on your performance with detailed accuracy metrics
- **Global Leaderboards**: Compete with players worldwide and climb the rankings
- **Personal Profiles**: Track your quiz history, favorite categories, and personal bests
- **Responsive Design**: Enjoy smooth gameplay on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Instant Feedback**: Review your answers and learn from detailed explanations

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15 with React and TypeScript
- **Backend**: Firebase Authentication and Firestore Database
- **Styling**: Modern CSS with responsive design principles
- **Deployment**: Optimized for performance on Vercel

## Perfect For
- Students looking to test their knowledge
- Trivia enthusiasts seeking new challenges
- Anyone wanting to learn while having fun
- Competitive players who love climbing leaderboards
